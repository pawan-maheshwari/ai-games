About AI Games

AI Games is a small, curated arcade from Planet AI, created by Pawan Maheshwari. Every title is a self-contained browser game — no downloads, no accounts. Open, play, learn.

The games turn real professional skills — retail operations, KYC compliance, business strategy — into short, playable challenges you can finish in a coffee break.

Our vision

The best way to learn a skill is to play it. We're building a growing arcade where serious workplace capability — rigor, compliance, judgment — is developed through joyful, game-shaped practice: built fast with AI, tested hard, and shared freely with learners and teams everywhere.

Privacy

This site sets no tracking cookies, runs no ads, and sells no data. Games run entirely in your browser; scores and progress stay on your device unless a game explicitly asks to share them (for example, QR multiplayer).

We follow privacy-by-design principles aligned with India's DPDP Act 2023. Questions? Reach out on LinkedIn.
